Golden Leaders
California has had many famous leaders. You can read a lot
about them. Two of the most famous ones were presidents.
Richard Nixon was born on a ranch in a city called Yorba
Linda. He became president in 1969. He was the only
president who ever resigned, which means that he left his
job as president early.
Ronald Reagan was born in the state of Illinois. But he
started acting and worked in Hollywood. Then he became
governor
of California. He became president in 1981.
There are libraries that honor these two presidents. Nixon’s
library is in Yorba Linda. Reagan’s library is in a city called
Simi Valley. Do you live close enough to visit?
