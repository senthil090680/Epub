FOLLOWING DIRECTIONS
The Golden State: Panning for Gold
What you will need:
• Small rocks
• Gold paint
• Paintbrush
• Aluminum pie pan/paper plate
• Sandbox
• Hand shovel
Directions:
1. Paint the small rocks gold and let them dry.
2. Have an adult poke a hole through the pie
pan or paper plate.
3. Hide the gold nuggets in a box of sand.
4. Scoop the sand into the pie pan or plate.
5. Gently shake the pie pan or plate back and
forth over the box of sand.
6. Pick out the gold rocks when you see them.
