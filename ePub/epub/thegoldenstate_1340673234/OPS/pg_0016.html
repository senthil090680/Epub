California is important to our country and the rest of the
world. The state produces many things for the whole nation.
California has big farms, ranches, and factories. Roads called
interstates help businesses take products to other states.
The state also has airports and seaports to ship products to
other nations. California businesses do a lot of
international
trade.
The state also has many international tourists. They visit
national parks like Yosemite or
amusement parks
like
Disneyland. They go on tours of Hollywood to see how
movies and TV shows are made. Visitors spend their
money in California when they eat and go from place
to place.
